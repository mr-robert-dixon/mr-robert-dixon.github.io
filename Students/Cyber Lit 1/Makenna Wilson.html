'Guide to Sexualities, Gender Identities, and Flags!''

Due to the recent legalization of gay marraige, people have begun to stand up and be proud of their sexuality and gender identities. This is admirable as people are being courageous and being proud of who they are after years of being oppresed. Now we have things such as Pride month, Pride parades, even pride flags! However sexuality and gender is known for being more of a spectrum rather than a simple 'one or the other.' For this reason, I created a guide to such sexualities and gender identities as well as their pride flags for the sake of education and acceptance.

Sexuality Description Flag
Homosexual/Gay A homosexual is someone who is sexually attracted to persons of the same sex,also known as 'gay'.
Bisexual A bisexual is someone is sexually attracted to people of 2 or more genders. It is also common to prefer one gender over another.
Pansexual A pansexuality is someone who is sexually attracted to people reagardless of their gender identity. This concept is known as 'gender blind'.
Lesbian A lesbian is someone who identifies as female and is sexually attracted to females.
Asexual An asexual is someone who is not sexually attracted to people regardless of their gender identity.
Demisexual A demisexual is someone who does not feel attraction until a strong bond has been created with that person.
Transgender Someone who is transgender is someone who identifies as a gender that is not the sex assigned to them at birth.
Genderfluid Someone who is genderfluid is someone who may fluctuate between identifying as male of female based on mood or feelings.
Non-Binary Someone who is non-binary does not identify as either male or female. Thus they dont identify as a binary gender as they feel that they are neither. These people often prefer they/them/their pronouns.
