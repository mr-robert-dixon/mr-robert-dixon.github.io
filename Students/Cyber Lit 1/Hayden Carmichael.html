RPGs

RPG's are also known as Role-Playing Games. In most to all of them you pick a class or role that is tailored to doing a specific thing. There are usually a wide variety of different playstyles to choose from so you can play the type of character you want. Each Role plays an important part in the game and teamwork is key. Also, another big element of RPGs is the huge world that you get to explore, with different enviroments and people to meet. These people could either be AI, or in the case of MMORPGs, real people.
