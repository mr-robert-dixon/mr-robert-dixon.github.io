Coding

As far as computer science goes my main interests lie in the code that makes things like video games, social media apps, smart tv's, and electronic devices in general work the way they do. I'm also very interested in building computers themselves and designing and creating computers.My last area of interest is AI Development. I think that AI in general is very interesting and freakishly dangerous but thats part of the reason I like it so much.

To start off I'd like to expand more on my interest of coding. Coding is essential in almost all computer run electronic devices such as phones, pc's, televisions, and even cars that have electronics in them to help them run like a GPS. To me it's very interesting because to most people it's either way too complicated for them to want to understand it or how it works or they just have no interest in it because it's boring to them. As a kid I thought it was extremely complicated as well however now that I have experience with both web design code and practical functional code I can say it's just as complicated as I thought it would be but that's also part of the reason I love it. To me coding is like solving a puzzle Especially practical code. You have to try different things over and over until something fits and makes everything else come together and work. And another thing I like about it is that it's completely freelance. If I want to write a code to make a robot spin in circles I can. If I want to make it go straight or turn or go backwards or even go in a specific pattern I can.
