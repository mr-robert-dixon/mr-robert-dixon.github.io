Bio-Food benefits

  • GMO crops can be tailored to provide better health benefits.
  • There is the possibility of an increased shelf life.
  • Genetically modified crops can conserve energy, soil, and water resources.
  • Fewer harmful agents need to be applied to crops.
  • Future GMOs could eliminate food allergies or intolerance issues.
  • There may be a positive environmental impact with GMO crops.
  • Farmers can use better ground-care methods.
